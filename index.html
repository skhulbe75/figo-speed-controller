Privacy Policy for YouTube Speed Controller Extension

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the YouTube Speed Controller extension ("the Extension") handles user data. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal or usage data. It operates entirely locally within your browser and does not transmit any information to external servers.

2. Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To interact with the currently active YouTube tab and control video playback speed.
  • Host permissions for https://www.youtube.com: To access YouTube and modify video playback speed.

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the Extension and do not involve data collection.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services or APIs that collect user data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
